Background

The dawn of the 25th of June 1998, i was introduced to this Massive Online  Multiplayer game ! I basically started from scratch, without any knowledge of neither the mechanics nor the objective of this game. Thankfully i spawned in a relatively middle class guild who, despite being a newbie, taught me the basics and rules of this huge world.
In this world, You don't  get to farm and grind to rise your level, as it rises at a constant rate, but its on you to catch up to it and make good use of your EXP !
At level 6; which is approximately 2190 Days after the day i spawned, I unlocked the "School", which was kinda common at that level mark.
At level 15; i tested the "Programming" skill, i loved it ! and immediately unlocked it and started to grind it whenever i could. 
At level 18; while i was at high school, I discovered many skills that were unlockable for me, notably "Drawing" and "Astronomy". 
When finally i maxed out the high school, and got my "Baccalaureate" achievement,  I had to choose a speciality, i didn't thought much about it by then, but i knew i loved programming so i narrowed down the options. But still; the ones i really wants had too much requirements that i couldn't accomplish at that time. So under the "College" category, i selected "ENSA" (Ecole National des Sciences Applique√©s in French) the National School of Applied Sciences, which has a Computer Science course, and grants an achievement of "State Engineer" after accumulating a total of 5 levels worth or knowledge and experience.
And finally, at level 19; i got introduced to Game Design. the best skill i unlocked so far ! and since then, i tried to learn as much as i could about it, and experiment with different technologies and tools, despite my time severely restricted my studies and academic projects. 

And here, after 23 levels, i managed to learn enough to create a couple of games, while others are still in progress. enough to teach me that there is far more interesting stuff to learn, and the best way to do so is by sharing with others and listening to their feedback :)
